I'll Give Him My All 1/2/21

Just wanted to share my motto from my mission: I'll give Him my all so that on that July day when I see you, my family, again, I'll be able to give you the biggest hug, then whisper in each of your ears, 'I gave Him my all'. I'll give everything so that on that day when I see you, my Savior, again, I'll be able to give you the biggest hug, then whisper in your ear, 'I gave YOU my all.'

Earn Your Pillow 12/25/20

I know what you're thinking -- your pillow?! Hear me out. If I know anything, it's that there's no greater satisfaction than going out every day and giving everything you have to the Lord. That way, when you get back at night and report on how your day went, you'll be able to hit your pillow reassured that you held nothing back and that you have deserved your pillow. Then wake up the next day ready to do it all again!
